FAQs

Answers to the some frequently asked questions.

How does this work?

+

We make it easy to sort and recycle your waste. You separate recyclable materials like plastics, paper, cans, and bottles. Then, you upload a photo of your sorted items via our app or website. We schedule a pickup, collect your recyclables, and reward you with tokens you can redeem for cash or products.

Is this service available in my area?

+

We're starting in select neighborhoods and cities in Nigeria. Join our waitlist to get notified when we launch in your area. Early supporters may also get access to exclusive rewards!

What kind of waste do you collect?

+

We currently accept the following recyclable waste: Plastic bottles and containers Paper and cardboard Aluminum cans Glass bottles Certain e-waste (on request) For now, we do not collect food waste, hazardous waste, or medical waste.

How do i use AI in the process?

+

Great question! Our platform uses AI to: Identify and verify recyclables via image recognition, Optimize pickup routes to reduce emissions, Recommend the best reward offers for you. This helps us operate efficiently and make the experience smoother for you.

Who handles the recycling?

+

We partner with trusted local recyclers and upcyclers across Nigeria. As we grow, we plan to build our own recycling and upcycling facilities to create more jobs and scale impact.

Is there a cost to join?

+

No, joining our platform and getting your waste picked up is completely free. We make money by working with sustainability partners and recycling partners.

Can businesses use this service

+

Absolutely! We work with restaurants, offices, hotels, and more. Businesses can also earn rewards and get sustainability credits. If you're a business interested in joining early, let us know when signing up.

What happens to waste after collection?

+

Your sorted waste is sent to our recycling or upcycling partners, who turn it into new products like packaging materials, furniture, or building components. Our goal is to return as much waste as possible into the economy

How do i join the waitlist?

+

Simply enter your email on the homepage and hit “Notify Me”. We'll email you when we're launching in your area and how to start earning rewards for your recyclables.
